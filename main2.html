Description

Do you know how many colors have you seen everyday? In our busy life, we tend to ignore the colorful world around us.
That's why we create

Let's C O L O R !

Enjoy it, and rediscover this colorful world!




Game

First:

A "color-collecting" mission will be assigned to you.



Next:

Look around, and see if you could find anything with the same color as the target.



Then:

Take a picture, and   Let's C O L O R !   will let you know if you match the color or not.


Share with your friends!

Store the result and create your own palette by these pictures. You might also want to share with your friends and see what picture they take for the same color.

Description

Do you know how many colors have you seen everyday?
In our busy life, we tend to ignore the colorful world around us.
That's why we create

C O L O R Finder

Enjoy it, and rediscover this colorful world!








How to play?

First :


A target color will be assigned to you.

Next :


Look around, and see if you could find anything with the same color as the target.

Then :


Take a picture, and the app will let you know how close you match the color.

Finally :


Now, you can share with your friends and see what pictures they've taken for the same color.
